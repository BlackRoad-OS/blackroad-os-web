## Design Philosophy

All app layouts follow these principles:

- **Three-column layout**: Sidebar | Main | Context
- **Dark by default**: #000 background, neon accents
- **Information density**: Power users want data, not whitespace
- **Keyboard-first**: Everything accessible via shortcuts
- **Cece always present**: AI assistant in right panel
